   - **Expert Reviewers' Suggested Change:** 
     - “Please consult your healthcare provider to discuss any specific medical concerns or before making changes to your treatment plan.”

   - **Reason for New Suggested Change:** Reinforcing the professional medical advice should be more impactful by including it in a prominent context within the document.

   - **New Suggested Sentence:** 
     - Add after the introduction paragraph: "Always consult your healthcare provider before making any changes to your treatment plan or if you have any specific medical concerns. Their guidance is crucial in ensuring that your treatment is adjusted safely and effectively."

   - **Deviation from Guidelines:** The source sentence does not sufficiently highlight the essential role of healthcare providers in managing treatment plans.

   - **Expert Reviewers' Suggested Change:** 
     - When you're living with type 2 diabetes, it’s important to regularly consult with your healthcare provider to reevaluate and adjust your treatment in response to your changing health needs.

   - **Reason for New Suggested Change:** Emphasize the collaborative role of healthcare providers and patients, enhancing the document's compliance.

   - **New Suggested Sentence:** 
     - "When you're living with type 2 diabetes, it's crucial to regularly consult with your healthcare provider to reassess and adjust your treatment plan based on your evolving health conditions. Your healthcare provider can help guide these changes to ensure they are safe and effective."
